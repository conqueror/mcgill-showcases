# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment
We are committed to a respectful, inclusive, and supportive learning environment for everyone.

## Expected Behavior
- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent and ask clarifying questions.
- Give feedback on ideas and code, not on people.
- Keep discussions welcoming to learners at different levels.

## Unacceptable Behavior
- Harassment, discrimination, or hostile language.
- Personal attacks, insults, or intimidation.
- Sharing private information without consent.

## Reporting
If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, open a private report using GitHub moderation tools or contact the maintainers through a private channel.

## Enforcement
Maintainers may remove content, limit participation, or take additional action to protect the learning environment.
