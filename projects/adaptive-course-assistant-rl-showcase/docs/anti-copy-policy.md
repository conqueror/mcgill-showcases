# Anti-Copy Policy

If you use this project for coursework, treat it as a pattern, not a template.

You may not reuse the following verbatim:

- the tutoring domain,
- the exact state variables,
- the exact action list,
- the reward function,
- the artifact structure,
- the evaluation write-up.

If you build your own assignment from this project, your job is to transfer the reasoning, not to rename the files and keep the same MDP.
